Our Mission

In this rapidly evolving digital landscape, Considerate Computing Group is dedicated to being a guiding light for businesses navigating the complexities of AI integration. As a team of passionate university students specializing in data science and AI, we strive to simplify and demystify the world of large language models in a friendly, approachable manner. Our mission is to empower businesses to harness the transformative power of AI, driving innovation, efficiency, and growth, while prioritizing data privacy and compliance. We are committed to building lasting partnerships with our clients, fostering a culture of continuous learning and adaptation in the face of ever-changing technological advancements.


Our Process

AI Opportunity Assessment

As an initial service, we analyze your business processes to identify opportunities to drive efficiency, innovation, and growth using AI-powered language solutions. This will allow us to create a roadmap and tailor our training and monitoring services to your organization's specific needs.

Tailored Employee Training

Based on the insights gained from our initial analysis, we provide customized training sessions for your employees, enabling them to effectively utilize AI-powered language models in their day-to-day tasks. Our approach ensures that your team feels confident and empowered to harness the full potential of these advanced technologies.

Technology Monitoring

In the rapidly evolving world of AI, it's crucial to stay ahead of the curve. We keep a close eye on emerging technologies and trends that could benefit your business, providing you with timely insights and recommendations to optimize your workflow.

Data Privacy & In-House AI

We understand the importance of data privacy and compliance with regulations like GDPR, especially for businesses handling sensitive data. Our team can guide you in choosing, implementing, and hosting open-source large language models in-house as an alternative to GPT-chat. This approach ensures your employees can confidently use AI solutions while safeguarding your valuable data and maintaining compliance.
